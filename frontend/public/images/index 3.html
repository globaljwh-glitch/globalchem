Welcome to GCC

Great Experience for Ingredients and Chemicals

Global Chemicals Corporation (GCC) is a leading provider of high quality natural and synthetic ingredients and specialty chemicals. With a strong legacy of innovation and excellence, we have become a trusted global partner in industries such as food and beverage, cosmetic and personal care, pharmaceuticals, and industrial cleaning.

At GCC, innovation drives our culture and operations. Our R&D Centers and Quality Control Labs in East Brunswick, New Jersey; Shanghai, China; and Ahmedabad, India reflect our commitment to emerging technologies. Backed by expert scientists and technical staff, we lead in Chemical, Cosmetic, Food, Nutrition, and Pharmaceutical industries. Our R&D investments and QA/QC labs ensure accurate validation of manufacturing partners' Certificates of Analysis. We strictly adhere to ISO 9001, ISO 14000, BRC, FSSC 22000, cGMP, and HACCP standards, following responsible care principles and full compliance with FDA, USDA, EPA, and all relevant regulations.

Beyond our BTS facilities, GCC fosters strategic partnerships with trusted global manufacturers to drive innovation and market expansion. Our business development teams provide both technical and commercial expertise, helping partners expand product lines and introduce new technologies. At GCC, we are committed to forming synergistic partnerships with our manufacturing partners and clients to enhance and strengthen their supply chains, improve cost efficiency, increase product profitability, and secure a competitive edge in both global and local markets. GCC delivers long-term, high-quality solutions for sustained success.

GCC has been forming long-term contractual partnerships with reputable builders in strategically located areas around the world to develop built-to-suit (BTS) facilities for manufacturing. A BTS facility is a custom-designed building created to meet the specific needs of a single tenant. In a BTS arrangement, the tenant collaborates with the developer or landowner to construct a new facility from the ground up, incorporating the tenant’s operational, spatial, and technological requirements into the design. Upon completion, the tenant leases the facility for a fixed term, typically ranging from 7 to 10 years, with options for renewal.

In our company culture, the customer is at the heart of everything we do. Our teams across R&D, Production, QA/QC, Customer Service, Logistics, and Distribution are all aligned with the needs of our customers. GCC’s foundation was built with the customer as our primary focus. We take a hands-on approach to deliver accurate, timely, and dependable service to all our clients. Our customer service team goes beyond being an operational extension—they act as advocates and strategic partners for each customer. As key stakeholders, they are dedicated to ensuring that every order is managed with professionalism and precision.
